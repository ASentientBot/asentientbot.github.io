Subcritical

A simple jailbreak tweak to override critical battery status; may help avoid random shutdowns in winter. Will not significantly extend runtime under regular circumstances.



In cold weather, my iPhone 4S usually dies by 60%. This tweak prevents that, roughly doubling battery life. Indoors, however, it only adds a couple extra minutes.

warnings

May accelerate battery degradation or cause data loss. Currently only tested on iOS 9.3.6. Use at your own risk.
